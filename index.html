About

Who We Are Founded in 2019 Maynooth Boxing club was started by John Donovan and his wife Marlene. Both John and Marlene have competed at amateur level and both represented Ireland at numerous world Championships. John and Marlene are both from Maynooth. When they began boxing training they had to travel to Dublin city as there were no local boxing clubs close by. Since both have retired from competitive boxing their goal has been to setup a local boxing club in Maynooth. This became a reality in 2018 and since then they opened a fully equipped boxing gym that provides classes for all ages and all skill levels. Whether your goal is to just get fit or learn the beautiful science of boxing this is the gym that will cater for you.They gym operates on a pay as you go system so there is no commitment at all required and prices range depending on the classes that you attend. The first class for everyone is free. So come on down or get in touch using our contact form and we will get back to you.

CLASSES

BOXFIT

Monday, Wednesay and Thursday at 5pm

Boxfit is a cardiovascular workout with classes lasting one hour. It is based on the training used for boxing so it includes skipping, boxing drills including footwork and abdominal workouts all while focusing on fitness and toning.

BOXING CLASSIC

Monday, Wednesay and Thursday at 7pm

A boxing-only class for individuals interested in developing their technical skills further. More in depth concentration on footwork, defensive skills and extensive combos. This class is designed to increase your strength and agility.

BOX LADIES

Tuesday and Friday at 7pm

This class is FOR ladies only. It is by no means less intense than the mixed classes and will take your conditioning to the next level. During these classes, the studio will be fully private and closed for ladies only. ​.

BOX KIDS

Saturday mornings at 10:30am

This class is for under 12's. The classes are very much aimed at having fun and gtting fit while also incorporating some basic self defense skills using boxing
